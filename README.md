## II. Core Reporting, Writing, and Editing Skills

| Subject | Why study? | Book | Videos |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **News Writing and Reporting** | Teaches story structure, sourcing, interviewing, and clarity. | **[Reuters Handbook of Journalism](https://handbook.reuters.com/)** (free, official online edition) | **[Become a Journalist: Report the News!](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/become-a-journalist)** (Coursera) |
| **Interviewing and Sourcing** | Builds evidence-based reporting through human sources. | **[Reuters Handbook](https://handbook.reuters.com/)** (Interviewing sections, free online) | **[An illustrated guide to the basics of interviewing](https://www.poynter.org/reporting-editing/2023/illustrated-guide-interviewing-basics-journalism/)** (Poynter) |

## III. Verification, Ethics, and Law

| Subject | Why study? | Book | Videos |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Verification and Fact-Checking** | Prevents misinformation and strengthens credibility. | **[Verification Handbook](http://verificationhandbook.com/)** (Silverman, ed., free online) | **[First Draft News: Verification Guides](https://firstdraftnews.org/articles/verifying-online-information-the-absolute-essentials/)** (essential guides and toolbox) |
| **Media Ethics and Accountability** | Explores professional responsibility and moral dilemmas. | **[Media Ethics: Issues and Cases](https://archive.org/details/mediaethicsissue0000unse_u1n4)** (Archive.org borrowing) | **[Media Ethics & Governance](https://www.coursera.org/learn/media-ethics-governance)** (Coursera, University of Amsterdam) |
| **Journalism Law and Regulation** | Explains legal constraints and protections for journalists. | **[Open University: Journalism Law](https://www.open.edu/openlearn/society-politics-law/starting-law/content-section-0)** (related OpenLearn law resources) | **[Journalism Law for the Digital Age](https://www.open.edu/openlearn/society-politics-law/starting-law/content-section-0)** (OpenLearn, related free modules) |
| **Journalist Safety and Risk Awareness** | Prepares journalists for physical, digital, and legal risks. | **[CPJ Journalist Safety Guide](https://cpj.org/security/guide/)** (free online) | **[CPJ Safety Training Videos](https://cpj.org/safety-kit/)** (various safety resources and notes) |
